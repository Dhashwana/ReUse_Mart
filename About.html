About ReUse Mart

Reuse Mart is a digital platform designed to connect households with nearby recyclers through an integrated online system. It enables users to list recyclable materials such as paper, plastic, glass, and metal, while allowing recyclers to view available listings and schedule pickups efficiently. Through structured interaction between users and recyclers, the platform simplifies the recycling process and promotes responsible waste management.

Our Objectives

  • To develop a digital platform that connects households with nearby scrap dealers and recyclers.
  • To make the recycling process simpler, structured, and more convenient for users.
  • To create awareness about recyclable materials and promote proper waste disposal practices.
  • To reduce landfill waste by encouraging recycling and reuse of materials.
  • To support responsible consumption and contribute towards a cleaner environment.

Project Contributions

  • Encourages recycling and reuse of materials.
  • Reduces environmental pollution.
  • Minimizes landfill waste.
  • Promotes sustainable lifestyle practices.
  • Supports circular economy initiatives.
